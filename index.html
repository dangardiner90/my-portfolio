About Me:

Hello! My name is Daniel, and thank you for taking the time to visit my website! I am currently a first year student at Humber Polytechnic studying Computer Programming & Analytics. My background is in network, security and home automation design, installation and programming. I'm an avid weightlifter, runner and yoga practitioner. I'm also a huge sci-fi and fantasy nerd, and obsessed with food and coffee.
